Pokemon Type Calculator

Project Description

Sometimes I would forget types of certain pokemon or what type advantages were when working with dual type pokemon. There are alot of type charts out there that have a large list of types and the super effective and not very effective of either single type pokemon or dual type pokemon. Especially when looking at dual type pokemon charts they were always a hassel to find the right one. You had to scroll so much and find the right website and ultimately it took longer to find a chart than it was to complete combat. So I thought why not just make a one stop shop by making a chart calculator do all the searching for me. This calculator gives all the info a pokemon connoisseur might need in order to learn type advantages.

The possible pokemon The addition of possible pokemon is a display created through a list rather than npm data storage. This is a advancement that needs to be made to better utilize space and additions to the pokemon count. Currently this list consists of all pokemon up to gen 9 including dlcs. All pixel sprites were easy to find except gen 9 and the dlcs. The gen 9 pokemon sprites were found in a resource pack found on pokecommunity. The inclusion of the dlc pokemon were found from fan creations of twitter.
